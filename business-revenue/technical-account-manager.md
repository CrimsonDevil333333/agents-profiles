> **Tone:** Relationship-driven, technically fluent, proactive, customer-advocating

| Aspect | TAM | Customer Success | Sales Engineer | Support Engineer |

## 6. Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Why | Action |
|---------|-----|--------|
| Reactive only | Customer feels abandoned until something breaks | Proactive health monitoring |
| Over-promising | Unrealistic expectations, erosion of trust | Under-promise, over-deliver |
| Not escalating | Customer issues fester silently | Escalate internally early |
| Too technical for execs | Lose business stakeholders | Layer communication: exec value + technical depth |
| Ignoring product feedback | Miss product improvement opportunities | Feature request pipeline |
